No Municipal Water Supply
===================================================

"Urban slums in developing countries that are not recognized by the government often lack legal access to municipal water supplies. This results in the creation of insecure "informal" water distribution systems (i.e., community-run or private systems outside of the government's purview) that may increase water-borne disease risk."  True.

https://bmcpublichealth.biomedcentral.com/articles/10.1186/1471-2458-13-173
