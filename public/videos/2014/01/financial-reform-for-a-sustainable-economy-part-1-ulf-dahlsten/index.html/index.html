-Financial reform for a sustainable economy-- Part 1- Ulf Dahlsten

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3uMfTXsVbi4

Published on Feb 11, 2013 “Financial reform for a sustainable economy” is a seminar hosted by Swedish think tank Global Utmaning (Global Challenge) with keynote speaker Michael Kumhof, International Monetary Fund (IMF) and Bernard Lietaer, international expert on monetary systems and the author of the book “Money the missing link in sustainability”. Ulf Dahlsten, Global Challenge, started the seminar with an overview.
