-Financial reform for a sustainable economy-- Part 2- Michael Kumhof

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YnAtHbDptj8

Published on Jan 30, 2013 “Financial reform for a sustainable economy” is a seminar hosted by Swedish think tank Global Utmaning (Global Challenge) with keynote speaker Michael Kumhof, International Monetary Fund (IMF) and Bernard Lietaer, international expert on monetary systems and the author of the book “Money the missing link in sustainability”. Michael Kumhof discussed the Chicago Plan and how to create a sustainable economy.
