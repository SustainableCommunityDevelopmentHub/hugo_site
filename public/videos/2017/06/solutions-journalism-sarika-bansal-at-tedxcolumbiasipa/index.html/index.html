TEDx Talks Published on Mar 7, 2013 SUBSCRIBE 7.8M SARIKA BANSAL is an independent journalist who specializes in social innovation and global health. She is a co-director of the Solutions Journalism Network, which supports rigorous and compelling journalism about responses to social problems. Sarika has previously worked in management consulting with McKinsey and in microfinance business development in India. She holds an undergraduate degree in Disease and Public Policy from Harvard College and a MPA from Columbia University’s School of International and Public Affairs.
